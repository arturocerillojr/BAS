What is Debt?

Debt is money borrowed by one party from another, typically under the agreement to repay with interest. It is a common financial tool used by individuals, businesses, and governments.

Types of Debt

  • Credit Card Debt: Revolving debt with high-interest rates.
  • Student Loans: Borrowed money to pay for education.
  • Mortgages: Long-term loans for purchasing property.
  • Auto Loans: Loans for buying vehicles.
  • Personal Loans: Unsecured loans for various uses.

Managing Debt

  • Make consistent, on-time payments.
  • Prioritize high-interest debt first.
  • Consolidate or refinance when appropriate.
  • Avoid taking on unnecessary new debt.
  • Create a budget and stick to it.
